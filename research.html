Degeneracy in the Dark Sector

Degeneracy between primordial non-Gaussianity and interaction in the dark sector on ultra-large linear scales

If dark energy and dark matter interact via exchange of energy and momentum, then this may affect the galaxy power spectrum on large scales. When this happens, it may be degenerate with the signal from primordial non-Gaussianity via a scale-dependent bias.We consider a class of interacting dark energy models and show that the matter overdensity is scale dependent on large scales. We estimate the effective non- Gaussianity arising from the large-scale effects of interaction in the dark sector. The signal of dark sector interaction can be disentangled froma primordial non-Gaussian signal bymeasuring the power at two redshifts. Reade more.
